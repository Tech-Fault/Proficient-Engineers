Proficient Engineers
EST. 2009 · VADODARA

Your Trusted Power Partner

Proficient Engineers is a Vadodara-based authorised silent genset dealership with over 15 years of experience in power protection solutions. We believe every business deserves uninterrupted, clean, and quiet power.

From initial consultation and equipment selection to installation, commissioning, and long-term AMC support, we are with our customers at every stage. Our certified engineers serve clients across Gujarat in sectors including manufacturing, healthcare, IT, and real estate.

With our motto "Let's Protect Electrical Systems", we go beyond just selling gensets — we ensure your entire electrical infrastructure stays safe, efficient, and always ready.
